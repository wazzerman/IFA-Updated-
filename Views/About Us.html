What Is ECO_?

The ECO_ concept represents the fundamental principles of Enviroment, Community and Organizations, it is a multisided platform that connects together two or more independent groups controlled in the interest of Oppurtunity value and impact to form ECO ambassadors , ECO leaders and ECO organizations. We work to share, create and sustain. Our platform allows groups to share knowledge and ideas with people and organizations around the globe with the intention to create, economic, social and enviromental opportunities, through sustainable impact.
