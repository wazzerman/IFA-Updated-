What Is ECO_?

ECO AFRICA our first project is a student lead socity in Universities that recognize that Africa, like many other parts of the world, suffer from social, economic and enviromental issues. The enviroment suffers disproportionate explotation and pollution, communities lack education basic amenities, social cohesion as well as other things adn organizations working from and within Africa are corrupted and non transparent.

ECO_AFRICA seeks to restore a balance, encourage transparency and create a better future for Africans. Connecting students locally to students internationally to change the world through collaboration.
